Hindi Lesson 1: Introduction to Hindi

This lesson will introduce you to the basics of Hindi, including the alphabet, pronunciation, and some basic phrases.

The Hindi Alphabet

The Hindi alphabet consists of 33 consonants and 13 vowels. The consonants are divided into five groups: gutturals, palatals, cerebrals, dentals, and labials.

The vowels are divided into two groups: short vowels and long vowels.

Pronunciation

Hindi is a tonal language, which means that the meaning of a word can change depending on the tone of voice used.

There are four tones in Hindi: high, mid, low, and falling.

Basic Hindi Phrases

Here are some basic Hindi phrases:

  • Namaste: Hello
  • Dhanyavaad: Thank you
  • Aapka naam kya hai?: What is your name?
  • Mera naam ... hai: My name is ...
